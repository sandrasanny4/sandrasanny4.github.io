You deserve a lover who wants you disheveled, with everything and all the reasons that wake you up in a haste and the demons you that won’t let you sleep. You deserve a lover who makes you feel safe, who can consume this world whole if he walks hand in hand with you; someone who believes that his embraces are a perfect match with your skin

You deserve a lover who wants to dance with you, who goes to paradise every time he looks into your eyes and never gets tired of studying your expressions You deserve a lover who listens when you sing, who supports you when you feel shame and respects your freedom; who flies with you and isn’t afraid tfall You deserve a lover who takes away the lies and brings you hope, coffee, and poetry.
